Events

1. How to register for an event and add it to your calendar

We run Seminars, Drop-in Sessions and other events during the year. Follow these steps to sign up and save the event to your calendar.

  1. Log in to the Hub at community.healthliteracyhub.org.au (opens in a new tab).
  2. Find the event. Go to the Events page, or look in the left sidebar under "Seminar Series" or "Upcoming Drop-ins".
  3. Click on the event title to open it. You will see the date, time and what the event is about.
  4. Click "RSVP" to save your spot. The page will then show "Going" with a green tick.
  5. Click "Add to calendar". You will find this button on the right side of the page, below "Going". Click it to download a calendar invite.
Events

2. How to join a live event after registering

After you register for an event, there are two ways to join when it starts.

  1. Check your email. You will get a reminder email before the event with a link to join. If you cannot find it, check your spam or junk folder.
  2. Or log in to the Hub and go to the Events page. When the event is live, a "Join live stream" button will show up on the right side of the event page.
  3. Click the "Join live room" or "Join live stream" button. Your browser will open the live session. All events use the built-in Live room or Live stream.
  4. Allow microphone and camera access if your browser asks. This is available for Live room events only. You can keep your camera off if you prefer.
Important: Make sure you are logged in to the Hub before you try to join. If you are not logged in, the join button may not show up. We suggest joining a few minutes early to check your connection.
Tip: All Hub events use the built-in Live Rooms or Live Streams. These work best in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox on a desktop or laptop.
Recordings

3. How to find recordings and summaries of past events

If you missed an event or want to look back at a session, you can find recordings and summaries on the Hub.

  1. Log in to the Hub at community.healthliteracyhub.org.au.
  2. Go to the right space in the sidebar. There are two main spaces for past content:
    "Seminar Recordings" in the Seminar Series group has video recordings of past seminars.
    "Past Drop-in Sessions" in the Research Drop-in Sessions group has written summaries of past sessions (not recordings).
  3. Browse or search. Past events are listed as posts. Seminar posts usually have the video, a summary, and any shared resources. Drop-in Session posts have a written summary and any resources or questions and answers.
  4. Click on a post to open it and see the content.
